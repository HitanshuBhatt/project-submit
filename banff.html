Majestic Banff and Lake Louise Tour Package

Stop 1: Lake Louise

Location: Lake Louise, Banff National Park, Alberta

Duration: 1 hour Be captivated by the turquoise waters of Lake Louise, framed by majestic mountain peaks. Experience scenic hiking trails, serene canoeing, and the tranquil beauty of this world-renowned destination.

Pass By: Vermilion Lakes

Location: Vermilion Lakes Rd, Banff, Alberta

Enjoy the peaceful charm of these reflective lakes, surrounded by stunning mountain vistas. This is a perfect opportunity for wildlife spotting and capturing breathtaking photographs.

Pass By: Castle Mountain

Location: Castle Mountain, Alberta

Marvel at the dramatic cliffs and towering slopes of Castle Mountain, a true testament to nature's raw power and beauty.

Stop 2: Johnston Canyon

Location: Bow Valley Parkway, Banff National Park, Alberta

Duration: 1 hour Discover the striking beauty of Johnston Canyon, featuring cascading waterfalls, turquoise pools, and towering cliffs. Traverse well-maintained trails and cross suspended bridges to immerse yourself in this awe-inspiring natural wonder.

Stop 3: Banff Town

Location: Banff, Alberta

Duration: 2 hours (including lunch at Banff Town) Explore the vibrant streets of Banff, lined with boutique shops and restaurants. Visit historic landmarks and enjoy the charm of this mountain retreat surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty.

Stop 4: Bow Falls

Location: Banff, Banff National Park, Alberta

Duration: 10 minutes Witness the Bow River tumbling gracefully over enchanting waterfalls. Take in the picturesque surroundings, capture unforgettable photographs, and enjoy nearby walking trails.

Final Stop: Calgary

Location: Calgary, Alberta

Duration: 2 hours (approximate arrival at 6:00 PM, subject to traffic) Conclude your tour with a comfortable journey back to Calgary, reflecting on the unforgettable natural beauty and experiences of the day.

Experience Highlights:

  • Iconic natural landmarks
  • Scenic hikes and photography
  • Wildlife spotting opportunities
  • Charming town exploration
